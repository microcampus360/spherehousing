As we continue to navigate the dynamic landscape of our industry, we are continually reminded of an invaluable lesson imparted by our founder: the importance of personal investment in our work and the people we collaborate with. This principle is not just a guiding philosophy, but a cornerstone of our culture. By fostering deeper connections with our work and one another, we can cultivate an environment where innovation thrives, ideas flourish, and resilience becomes second nature. Together, we can build a future that reflects our shared dedication, passion, and vision.

Dr. Harish Aithal

FOUNDER & MANAGING DIRECTOR

Dr. Harish Aithal, the visionary founder of SPHERE HOUSING, exemplifies the ideals of integrity, innovation, and community engagement. His profound commitment to fostering human connections and enhancing environmental sustainability serves as the cornerstone of the organization’s ethos. His distinguished career is characterized by a human-centric vision that prioritizes the needs and aspirations of clients, alongside a dedicated focus on creating spaces that enrich lives.

With a background steeped in architectural excellence and a passion for sustainable development, Dr. Aithal has consistently championed quality and craftsmanship in every project undertaken by SPHERE HOUSING. His personalized approach to both projects and people not only underscores his leadership style but also cultivates an atmosphere where collaboration and creativity thrive.

Dr. Aithal’s relentless pursuit of technological innovation has positioned SPHERE HOUSING at the forefront of the industry, enabling the organization to adapt and respond to the evolving needs of the market while maintaining an unwavering commitment to sustainability. Under his stewardship, SPHERE HOUSING continues to be a beacon of excellence in the housing sector, driven by a mission that aligns profitability with purpose.

Dr. Harish Aithal is not just a founder; he is a catalyst for change, dedicated to building a better future for all.

Our Team

At the heart of our organization is a diverse and dynamic team, united by a shared passion for creativity and innovation. We come from various backgrounds as creative, visionaries, engineers, architects, and leaders, each bringing a unique perspective and skill set that enriches our collaborative spirit.

Our mission is clear: to create meaningful milestones in our own journeys and in the lives of others. We are committed to fostering an environment where workforce happiness and creative satisfaction are paramount. Success for us is not measured by numbers or metrics; instead, we continually strive to surpass our own standards, focusing on sculpting a brighter future.

We believe that our workforce is our greatest asset, and we are dedicated to cultivating a team that thrives on enthusiasm, talent, and a commitment to growth—both personal and professional. Our quirky team is thoughtfully assembled, precisely because we value diverse talents and a willingness to learn and unlearn.

Together, we aspire to push boundaries, challenge conventions, and make a lasting impact in our industry and beyond. Join us as we embark on this journey of creativity, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

Our team takes over everything, from an idea and concept development to realization.
